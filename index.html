Icebucket challenge goes viral

How many people are reached before time $T$?

In the background we see a visualization of the spreading of the Ice Bucket Challenge in the summer of 2019. In this challenge that went viral on Facebook, you had to make a video where you had to throw a bucket of icewater over your head, OR donate some money to charity. We see that after a slow start in May, the spread goes quicker and quicker. In only a few months it was able to spread all over the world and billions got acquainted with it.

More spreading in networks

There are many more real-world examples where spread in networks is present: diseases in the real-world, computer viruses over the internet, and more recently fake news has attracted much attention.
